## 🎨 Customization Quick Guide

### Change Company Name
**File:** `components/header.tsx` (line 50)
- Just replace "TechVision" with your company name

### Change Colors
**File:** `app/globals.css` (lines 65-90)
- Light mode: `--primary`, `--accent`
- Dark mode: `.dark { --primary, --accent }`

### Update Content
- Hero: `components/hero.tsx`
- Features: `components/features.tsx`
- Products: `components/products.tsx`
- Team: `components/team.tsx`
